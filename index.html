You will be given a random score at the start of the game.

There are four football helmets below. By clicking on a football helmet you will add a specific amount of total points to your scrore.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random score, you lose the game if your total goes above that random score.

The value of each football helmet is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each football helmet.
