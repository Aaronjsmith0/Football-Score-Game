You will be given a random Guest score at the start of the game.


There are four football helmets below. By clicking on a football helmet you will add a specific amount of total points to the home scrore.


You win the game by matching the home score to the guest score, you lose the game if your total goes above that guest score.


The value of each football helmet is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each football helmet.
